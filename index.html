Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! Give us a call at (402) 875-1369 and we'll provide a free estimate based on your vehicle's condition and the services you're interested in.

What areas do you serve?

We're located in Lincoln, Nebraska and serve the entire Lincoln area. Visit us at 122 S Antelope Valley Pkwy, Lincoln, NE 68510.

How long does detailing take?

It depends on the service. A basic wash and wax might take a couple hours, while a full interior and exterior detail can take 4-6 hours. We'll give you a time estimate when you book.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and debit cards for your convenience.

Do you come to my location?

We operate from our Lincoln location where we have all our equipment and supplies. You can drop off your vehicle or wait while we work if you prefer.

How often should I detail my car?

For best results, we recommend a full detail every 3-6 months depending on how you use your vehicle. Regular maintenance keeps your car looking great and protects your investment.
